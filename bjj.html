Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu: The Gentle Art

Why I Love BJJ

What started as a way to stay fit while also learning a skill, has evolved into a passion for the art and the sport. I enjoy learning new techniques and seeing how the apply to different scenarios. I encourage everyone who has an opportunity to take a class and see if it is for them.

The BJJ Journey

  1. White Belt Fundamentals

    • Understanding hip movement
    • Developing body awareness
  2. Blue Belt Development
    • Guard retention
    • Submission defense
    • Basic combinations
  3. Advanced Training
    • Competition preparation
    • Advanced guard work
    • Teaching fundamentals
